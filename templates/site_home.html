[ 05 / 06 ]  STRAIGHT ANSWERS Straight answers

The questions every contractor asks.

Will it post things I didn't approve?

No. On Pro, JobMagnet drafts everything and you approve every send. On Premium it can act on its own for your own channels — social, your Google listing, review requests to your own past customers — but it never sends a cold text or email without a rule you set or a one-tap okay. Your customers, your call.

I'm not a computer person. Is this hard?

There's no dashboard to learn. JobMagnet interviews you once in plain English, then tells you each morning what it did and what booked. If you can text, you can run JobMagnet.

How is this different from an agency?

An agency charges $2,000–3,000 a month, works 9-to-5, and doesn't know your trade. JobMagnet costs a fraction, works around the clock, speaks the trades, and proves every dollar in cost per booked job. No agency hits all four.

Does it actually book jobs or just post?

Booking work is the whole job. JobMagnet is measured on cost per booked job, not posts made. Social and reviews create the demand; speed-to-lead and outreach help capture it; the dashboard proves it.

What about spam and the rules?

Compliance is built into the code, not bolted on. Cold texting and AI voice stay hard-off until there's written consent and an attorney sign-off. Marketing email carries a real address and a working unsubscribe. JobMagnet keeps you out of the ditch.
