Good to know

Pricing questions, answered straight.

What's the difference between Pro and Premium?

On Pro, JobMagnet advises — it drafts content, review requests and lead replies, and you approve every send. On Premium, JobMagnet is unleashed: it acts on its own across your owned channels, and still asks before any cold or regulated outreach. Same JobMagnet, more rope.

Is there really a free start?

Yes. You can run the walkthrough, get your game plan, and see JobMagnet work without a card. You only pay when you're ready to let it send on your behalf.

Can I change plans later?

Anytime, both directions. Upgrading feels like unleashing the JobMagnet you already trust, not buying a second product.

How does the texting line work?

You connect a texting number (Twilio) in Connections, and once it's linked JobMagnet sends review requests and lead replies for real, within your monthly allowance. Until you connect a number, those texts are simulated so you can see exactly what would go out before anything sends.
